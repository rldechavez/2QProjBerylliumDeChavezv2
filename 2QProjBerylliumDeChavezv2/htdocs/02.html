Architecture

What is Architecture?

Architecture is the art and science of designing buildings and other physical structures. A wider definition often includes the design of the total built environment from the macro level of town planning, urban design, and landscape architecture to the micro level of construction details and, sometimes, furniture.

Architecture is the built realisation of a particular concept, or idea. This idea can be about construction, or the way people will use a building, or how thee building fits into a physical, or social landscape.

Architecture types? Yes, such thing exists. This means the purpose for creating that architecture. Designing a building for its specific purpose.

Domestic Architecture
- is produced for the social unit: the individual, family, or clan and their dependents, human and animal. This type of architecture mainly provides shelter and security for the basic physical functions of life and at times also for commercial, industrial, or agricultural activities.
Religious Architecture
- is concerned more with religious buildings such as churches, temples, shrines, mosques, and etcetera.
Governmental Architecture
- architecture that is concerned with the buildings of used by government officials (e.g. Malacanang palace, The White House)
Recreational Achitect
- focuses on creating architectures foir hobbies (e.g. Theatres, Auditoriums, Athletic facilities, Museums, and Libraries)
Architecture of welfare and education
- creating facilities that provide education, health, public security, and utilities.

Architectural styles can be observed on the design of the building. The list below are some architectural styles we often see in Philippines.

Modern
- an architectural style based upon new and innovative technologies of construction, particularly the use of glass, steel, and reinforced concrete; the idea that form should follow function (functionalism); an embrace of minimalism; and a rejection of ornament. More on Modern Architecture.
Neoclassical
- Neoclassical architecture is an architectural style produced by the Neoclassical movement that began in the mid-18th century in Italy and France which then became one the most prominent and iconic architectural styles in the Western World.
Ranch-style house
- Ranch is a domestic architectural style originating in the United States. The ranch-style house is noted for its long, close-to-the-ground profile, and wide open layout.
Timber Framing
- is a distinctive style of building construction in which heavy timbers frame the structure instead of more slender dimensional lumber.
