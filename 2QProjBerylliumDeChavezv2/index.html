What is Arts?

Arts is the expression or application of human creative skill, and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture. Some art forms combine a visual element with performance (e.g. cinematography), or artwork with the written word (e.g. comics). From prehistoric cave paintings to modern-day films, art serves as a vessel for storytelling and conveying humankind's relationship with the environment.
